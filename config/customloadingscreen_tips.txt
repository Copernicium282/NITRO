Connect with other players on the official Discord server, which can be accessed from the Main Menu.
Contribute or report bugs on GitHub.
Use the JEI search bar to find any recipe or item info.
Refer to the quest book for guidance (note that it is a work in progress).
Machines produce visible pollution — plan your factory layout accordingly.
Never explore without good armor, weapons, and a plan for retreat.
Research any SCPs you encounter, but proceed with extreme caution.
You cannot craft a Nether Portal; dig down to reach the Nether instead.
Dig to bedrock to access The Beneath.
Never build multiblocks on chunk borders — they will break.
If something feels too tedious, you are probably doing it wrong. Check JEI.
Automate tasks as early as possible to save time in the long run.
The SILEX machine reprocesses depleted nuclear fuel — the Fuel Reprocessor is disabled for most fuels.
SILEX recipes require Nitric Acid and a Laser Crystal (CO₂ for LEU, Bismuth for HEU).
Craft Nitric Acid using the Catalytic Chemistry Reactor: Nitrogen → Nitric Oxide → NO₂ → Nitric Acid.
All Chemistry Reactor recipes require a Catalyst, made in the NC Assembler.
HBM and NuclearCraft share plates — the NC Pressurizer outputs HBM plates for Iron, Gold, Titanium, Copper, Aluminum, and Steel.
Steam Condensers (Mk I–III) convert exhaust steam back to water. Build one early for your turbines.
Basic Plating, Solenoids, and Electric Motors are crafted at the HBM Anvil, not in the crafting table.
The Decay Hastener is an endgame item — it requires Solinium nuggets and Euphemium.
Solinium fuels come in three enrichment tiers: HES, MES, and LES. Made from HBM Solinium billets.
Rocket engines and fuel tanks use HBM thrusters and fuselages — check JEI for the new recipes.
The Warp Core requires Schrabidium blocks — save your Schrabidium for space travel.
Use Iron Backpacks for extra inventory space.
Build radiation protection pills early: Iodine Pills, FNZP Tablets, and Radaway are craftable.
For serious radiation, craft Strong Radaway or Radaway Flush for greater decontamination.
Speed and Energy Upgrades for NuclearCraft machines require the Researcher machine.
Modular Machinery hatches come in 7 tiers: upgrading requires the previous tier + circuits.
Jetpacks start at Copper tier — the Wood and Stone tiers are disabled.
Allocate at least 4 GB of RAM and lower render distance for better FPS.

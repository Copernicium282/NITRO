§6Dimensional Science: Customizing with Dimlets§f

§aInject customized coding parameters into your dimension tabs to dictate block generation, fluids, and time!§f
Random dimensions are volatile and unpredictable. To construct highly specific worlds—such as worlds made of pure Diamond Blocks or oceans of Liquid Gold—you must learn to manufacture and arrange specialized programming components called §9Dimlets§f.

§6Dimlet Workbench§f:
§eThe Dimlet Workbench is the central workshop for dimlet engineering§f. Use it to:
§b• Deconstruct Dimlets§f: Break down unwanted dimlets found in dungeons or Dimension chests to extract valuable components: §bDimlet Memory Chips§f, §bDimlet Control Chips§f, and §bDimlet Energy Cores§f.
§b• Craft Dimlets§f: Combine these structural chips with a specific base item (such as a Diamond Block for material, or a bucket of water for fluid) to manufacture custom dimlets.
§b• Analyze Parcels§f: Open §bDimlet Parcels§f gathered during exploration to discover random, rare dimlet templates.

§6Programming Order Rules§f:
§cThe order of dimlets inside the Dimension Enscriber is extremely important!§f You must place modifier dimlets (such as Material or Fluid) immediately §ebefore§f the target feature dimlet (such as Terrain).
  - *Example*: Place a §bDiamond Block Material Dimlet§f immediately followed by a §bFlat Terrain Dimlet§f to enscribe a world made of solid diamond flats! Failure to arrange them correctly will result in a chaotic, scrambled world layout.

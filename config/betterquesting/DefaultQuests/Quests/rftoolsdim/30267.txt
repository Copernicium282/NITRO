§6Dimensional Science: Hazards & Forcefields§f

§aPrevent catastrophic world collapses and secure your personal safety with advanced forcefields!§f
Creating custom dimensions is an unstable science. Richer, high-tier dimensions (like those containing valuable ores or infinite fluids) consume massive volumes of RF energy per tick. If your base's power grid fails and the Dimension Builder runs dry, the pocket world collapses instantly, dealing lethal damage to anyone inside.

§6World Collapse Hazards§f:
§cIf the Dimension Builder reaches 0 RF§f, the active dimension enters a power failure state. The atmosphere becomes instantly toxic, inflicting rapid, lethal void damage on all players inside. Without emergency equipment, you will suffocate and die within seconds, losing your items inside the collapsed world.

§6Phased Field Generator (PFG)§f:
§eThe PFG is your ultimate personal safety net!§f Keep this charged handheld device in your inventory whenever traveling to custom dimensions:
§b• Energy Buffer§f: Stores up to §e1,000,000 RF§f of internal energy.
§b• Active Shielding§f: If the dimension collapses, the PFG automatically triggers a localized personal forcefield, blocking all lethal collapse damage at a cost of §c100 RF/tick§f! This gives you a crucial window of time to use a teleporter or escape back home.
§b• Charging§f: Charges up to §a1,000 RF/tick§f inside standard RF chargers.

§6Activity Probe§f:
§eTo prevent offline power drains§f, craft and place an §bActivity Probe§f inside your custom dimension. The probe halts the dimension's power consumption completely when no players are active inside, keeping your main base's energy reserve perfectly safe while you are offline or elsewhere.

§6Dimensional Science: Creating Worlds§f

§aPlay God and construct customized, pocket dimensions tailored to your base's exact needs!§f
The §9RFTools Dimensions§f mod allows you to write, construct, and sustain entirely new dimensions. By registering world parameters onto digital templates and powering massive build arrays, you can teleport into custom dimensions to extract rare ores, harness massive energy reservoirs, or build sprawling factories.

§6Dimension Construction Steps§f:
§b1. Enscribe the Tab§f: Place an §bEmpty Dimension Tab§f inside the §9Dimension Enscriber§f. To make a random, default dimension, simply hit write without adding any dimlets.
§b2. Construct the World§f: Place the resulting §bRealized Dimension Tab§f inside the §9Dimension Builder§f.
§b3. Power the Array§f: The Dimension Builder requires massive streams of energy to build and sustain the world. It has a massive capacity of §e10,000,000 RF§f and can receive up to §a50,000 RF/tick§f.
§b4. Teleport§f: Set up a §bMatter Transmitter§f at your base and a §bMatter Receiver§f inside the dimension to establish a wireless wormhole link, allowing you to teleport into your new world!

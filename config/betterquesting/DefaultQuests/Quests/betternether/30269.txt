§6Ecology of the Underworld: Better Nether§f

§aExplore a living, breathing Nether filled with unique flora, biomes, and foreign wood resources!§f
The Nether is no longer just a barren wasteland of red netherrack. It is now home to diverse ecosystems, plant life, and structural resources.

§6Nether Biomes§f:
§b• Mushroom Forests§f: Sprawling regions covered in nether mycelium, giant orange/red mushrooms, spores, and mosses.
§b• Nether Grasslands§f: Flat, open plains covered in sparse nether grass and poor grassland flora.
§b• Gravel Deserts§f: Dry, desolate plains consisting of vast gravel beds and hostile cactus growth.
§b• Bone Reefs§f: Desolate canyons littered with ancient bones, rib cages, and dry bone formations.

§6Stalagnate & Nether Flora§f:
§b• Stalagnate Columns§f: Pillar-like woody plants that grow from the floor to the ceiling. Harvest the §bStalagnate Stem§f and §bStalagnate Bark§f to craft §bStalagnate Planks§f for building.
§b• Stalagnate Bowls & Stews§f: Craft §bStalagnate Bowls§f to brew nutritious local meals such as §bMushroom Stew§f, §bApple Stew§f, and §bWart Stew§f.
§b• Nether Reed§f: Found growing near lava pools. Harvest these to craft ladders, fences, gates, and doors.
§b• Agriculture§f: Collect §bWart Seeds§f, §bBlack Apple Seeds§f, and §bInk Bush Seeds§f to cultivate Nether crops.

§cWarning: Dangerous Vegetation§f:
§cWatch your step!§f The §bEgg Plant§f is a hazardous crop that §cdamages both players and mobs§f upon contact. Treat it with the same caution as a cactus.

§6Setting Your Spawn§f:
§eAnchor yourself to the Nether§f! The §bPig Statue Respawner§f acts as a spawn anchor in the underworld. Right-click the statue to set your respawn point directly inside the Nether.

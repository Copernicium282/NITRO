§6Underworld Metallurgy: Cincinnasite§f

§aForge advanced tools and ornate structures from the Nether's native golden ore!§f
Beyond simple wood and mushroom farming, the Nether contains a valuable native metal known as §bCincinnasite§f.

§6Mining & Processing§f:
§b• Cincinnasite Ore§f: A metallic mineral that spawns embedded in netherrack throughout the dimension.
§b• Smelting§f: Smelt the ore to obtain raw §bCincinnasite§f, which serves as the primary component for nether-indigenous metallurgy.

§6Tool Upgrades & Reinforcement§f:
§b• Standard Tools§f: Craft the basic §bCincinnasite Pickaxe§f and §bCincinnasite Axe§f for moderate mining and harvesting capabilities.
§b• Diamond-Reinforced Tools§f: Combine your standard tools with diamonds to forge the §bDiamond-Reinforced Cincinnasite Pickaxe§f and §bDiamond-Reinforced Cincinnasite Axe§f. This upgrade significantly §aboosts durability and harvesting speed§f.

§6Cincinnasite Architecture§f:
§eBeautify your base with ornate metal blocks§f! You can shape Cincinnasite into a wide range of structural blocks:
§b• Blocks & Columns§f: Columns, pedestals, and fire bowls.
§b• Bricks & Tiles§f: Small/large tiles, bricks, and stairs.
§b• Decorative§f: Carved pillars, frames, walls, buttons, and doors.
§b• Lighting§f: Ornate §bCincinnasite Lanterns§f.

§6Nether Cities§f:
§eExplore ancient monuments§f! Massive §bNether Cities§f generate infrequently throughout the dimension (every §e80 chunks§f). These grand structures are built primarily of Cincinnasite blocks and are filled with chests containing rare treasures, but expect heavy resistance from the guardians patrolling the city walls.

§6Tiered Storage: Backpacks§f

§aUpgrade your inventory space and take your base's resources with you on long expeditions!§f
The §9Iron Backpacks§f mod introduces tiered, wearable, and highly customizable backpack storage. As you progress from basic materials to diamonds, you can expand both your inventory slots and the capacity to equip specialized functional upgrades.

§6Backpack Progression & Specialties§f:
§b• Basic Backpack§f: The entry-level tier. Provides §e18 slots§f of storage and supports up to §a4 upgrade points§f. Cannot be upgraded with specialties.
§b• Iron Backpack§f (Tier 1): Base of §a7 upgrade points§f.
  - §bStorage Specialty§f: Configured with §e36 slots§f and §a7 upgrade points§f.
  - §bUpgrade Specialty§f: Configured with §e27 slots§f and §a12 upgrade points§f (+5 bonus).
§b• Gold Backpack§f (Tier 2): Base of §a12 upgrade points§f.
  - §bStorage Specialty§f: Configured with §e45 slots§f and §a12 upgrade points§f.
  - §bUpgrade Specialty§f: Configured with §e36 slots§f and §a17 upgrade points§f (+5 bonus).
§b• Diamond Backpack§f (Tier 3): Base of §a18 upgrade points§f.
  - §bStorage Specialty§f: Configured with §e54 slots§f (maximum!) and §a18 upgrade points§f.
  - §bUpgrade Specialty§f: Configured with §e45 slots§f and §a23 upgrade points§f (maximum upgrade capacity, +5 bonus).

§6Nesting Restrictions§f:
§cTo prevent critical NBT data bloat and server lag§f, the config limits backpack nesting to a maximum of §c1 level deep§f (§bmaxNests=1§f). You cannot nest multiple filled backpacks inside each other recursively without exceeding this hard safeguard.

§6Crafting & Upgrading§f:
§b• Basic Recipe§f: Surround a §bChest§f in the center with §b4× Wool§f in the corners and §b4× Leather§f on the cardinal sides.
§b• Specialty Branching§f: When upgrading to higher tiers, you must choose your focus:
  - §aStorage focus§f uses a §bChest§f in the top-center recipe slot to maximize inventory size.
  - §aUpgrade focus§f uses a §bBlank Upgrade§f in the top-center recipe slot to maximize upgrade points.

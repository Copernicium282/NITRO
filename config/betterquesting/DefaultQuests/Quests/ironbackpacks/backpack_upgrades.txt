§6Backpack Upgrade System§f

§aCustomize your backpack's behavior to automatically filter, organize, and manage items on the fly!§f
Iron Backpacks are not just simple inventory expansion blocks; they feature a powerful, modular upgrade system. By applying functional upgrades to your backpacks, you can automate item retrieval, prevent damage loss, and tailor your storage exactly to your survival needs.

§6Applying Upgrades§f:
§9Shift + Right-click§f the backpack in your hand or access its GUI to view the upgrade slot interface. Each upgrade consumes a specific number of §aUpgrade Points§f. You can equip as many upgrades as your backpack's tier and specialty max points limit allows!

§6Available Upgrades§f:
§b• Blank Upgrade§f: The foundational template used to craft all functional upgrades. Crafted with §e4× String§f (corners), §e4× Paper§f (cardinals), and §e1× Stick§f (center).
§b• Latch Upgrade§f: Keeps the backpack's contents strictly secure and locked. Crafted with §e4× Gold Ingots§f, §e4× String§f, and §e1× Blank Upgrade§f in the center.
§b• Damage Bar Upgrade§f: Displays a visual durability/charge bar directly on the backpack icon, indicating how full it is without needing to open the GUI. Crafted with §e4× Bowls§f, §e4× String§f, and §e1× Blank Upgrade§f.
§b• Extra Upgrade Point§f: Adds an additional §a+1 Upgrade Point§f to the backpack, letting you squeeze extra functionality out of lower-tier bags. Crafted with §e4× Leather§f, §e4× String§f, and §e1× Blank Upgrade§f.

§cNote§f: §eThe Everlasting Upgrade has been disabled§f in this pack's configuration. Plan your upgrades around the remaining functional modules!

§6Modular Kitchen: Core Architecture§f

§aConstruct a unified cooking network and become a master chef in your own home!§f
The §9Cooking for Blockheads§f mod allows you to build a functional, modular kitchen multiblock. By placing specialized kitchen blocks adjacent to each other, you form a connected inventory network that the §9Cooking Table§f accesses to display every meal you can craft with your available ingredients.

§6Core Kitchen Modules§f:
§b• Cooking Table§f: The brain of the kitchen. Place it adjacent to your storage blocks. Opening its GUI displays all recipes you can make using ingredients anywhere in your kitchen, allowing instant, single-click crafting!
§b• Fridge§f: High-capacity storage specifically designed for food items.
§b• Oven§f: Automatically cooks food placed inside it.
  - §cFuel Penalty§f: In this pack, fuel in the Oven lasts only §c33% (1/3rd)§f as long as in a vanilla furnace! It is highly recommended to upgrade to a §9Heating Unit§f early on.
§b• Sink§f: An essential utility for water-heavy recipes (like dough).
  - §cConfig Warning§f: The sink §cdoes not provide infinite water automatically§f in this modpack! You §cmust pipe water into the back or bottom§f of the Sink using pipes/cables for it to function.

§6Storage & Layout§f:
§eAny kitchen blocks placed adjacent to each other link automatically§f. Use §bKitchen Counters§f, §bKitchen Cabinets§f, and §bKitchen Corners§f to add stylish decoration and massive dry-storage capacity to your network.

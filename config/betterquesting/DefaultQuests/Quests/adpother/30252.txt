§6Environmental Hazard: The Three Pollutants§f

§aWatch the skies and tread lightly: your industrial footprint is actively poisoning the world!§f
The §9Pollution of the Realms§f mod simulates realistic environmental emissions. Running combustion machines, smelting ores, and burning coal releases toxic gas clouds that accumulate in chunks. Left unmanaged, they color the sky in thick smog and inflict severe, compounding physical debuffs.

§6The Three Pollutants§f:
§b• Carbon§f (Black): Concentrates in the upper skies (§ealt 127§f). Direct contact causes §cBlindness§f. Accumulated chunk levels trigger §cMining Fatigue (30%)§f, §cHunger (40%)§f, §cWeakness (50%)§f, §cSlowness (60%)§f, §cPoison (70%)§f, §cNausea (80%)§f, and §cWither (90%)§f. Mobs in polluted chunks get buffed with §aStrength§f and §aRegeneration§f!
§b• Sulfur§f (Yellow): Concentrates in the skies (§ealt 127§f). Direct contact causes §cBlindness§f and §cPoison§f. Chunk levels trigger §cPoison (30%)§f, §cBlindness (50%)§f, and §cWither (80%)§f. Mobs in polluted chunks get buffed with §aResistance§f and §aSpeed§f!
  - §cAcid Rain & Corrosion§f: Above §c5% Sulfur§f, standard rain turns to §cAcid Rain§f, corroding natural stone into §bCobblestone§f and permanently decaying grass into §bPodzol§f.
§b• Dust§f (White): Settles at ground/mining level (§ealt 0§f). Direct contact causes §cBlindness§f. Greatly impacts mining operations.

§6Explosion Risks§f:
§cPollutants are highly volatile!§f If an ignition source (torches, fire, or explosions) touches a large cluster of gas blocks, it will trigger an explosive chain reaction up to §c512 blocks long§f. The lower explosive limits are:
§b• Carbon§f: §c10 adjacent blocks§f
§b• Sulfur§f: §c16 adjacent blocks§f
§b• Dust§f: §c20 adjacent blocks§f

§eTo read exact chunk statistics§f, craft an §bAerometer§f to display active percentages for each pollutant.

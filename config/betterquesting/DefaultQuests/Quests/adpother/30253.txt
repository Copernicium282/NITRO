§6Base Management: Chimneys & Active Cleanup§f

§aPrevent toxic buildup inside your base and filter harmful pollutants before they choke you!§f
Industries cannot be run in closed basements without immediate suffocation. To prevent your factory from filling with carbon and sulfur smog, you must install functional exhaust grids and active cleanup systems to filter the atmosphere.

§6Advanced Chimneys§f:
§b• Chimneys & Vents§f: Crafted in massive stylistic varieties (Cobblestone, Brick, Bedrock, etc.) depending on your base aesthetics. Place them directly on top of or connected to fuel-burning blocks (like furnaces, steam boilers, or coal engines).
§b• Venting Grid§f: Chimneys conduct smoke vertically and vent it safely up to the high sky (§ealt 127§f), preventing carbon clouds from pooling inside your base's working rooms.

§6Active Filtering & Vacuuming§f:
§b• Vacuum Tube & Vacuum Bag§f: An active personal cleanup tool. Hold the vacuum cleaner in your hand and right-click to physically suck up accumulating pollutant blocks in the air. Sucked up pollutants are stored in the vacuum bag as useful raw byproducts:
  - §bCarbon§f blocks yield §bCoal/Carbon Dust§f.
  - §bSulfur§f blocks yield §bSulfur Dust§f.
  - §bDust§f blocks yield §bClay Balls§f!
§b• Pollutant Pump§f: An automatic, automated machine block. Connect power and pipes to the Pollutant Pump to suck up and filter all surrounding air blocks in a large radius, keeping your base's atmosphere perfectly clean automatically.

§6Personal Protection: Respirators§f

§aProtect your lungs from toxic industrial smog with specialized breathing gear!§f
As your base expands and you smelt more materials, chunks will accumulate thick layers of Carbon, Sulfur, and Dust. Walking through these polluted zones unprotected will quickly kill you. Crafting a §9Respirator§f is an absolute necessity to safely navigate and work around active factories.

§6Personal Respirators§f:
§b• Iron Respirator§f: The entry-level breathing mask. Worn in the head slot.
§b• Golden Respirator§f: Offers moderate armor value and slightly higher filter durability.
§b• Diamond Respirator§f: The ultimate breathing mask, providing top-tier armor protection and maximum filter capacity.

§6Filter Materials & Maintenance§f:
§eRespirators require filter materials to actively purify the air§f. As you breathe, these filters deplete. You can construct filter inserts out of various natural fibers:
§b• treeLeaves§f: Capacity of §a8 blocks§f (yields §bCarbon/Coal Dust§f as byproduct).
§b• wool§f (Any color): Capacity of §a8 blocks§f (yields §bSulfur Dust§f as byproduct).
§b• paper§f: Capacity of §a8 blocks§f (yields §bClay Ball§f as byproduct).

§cWarning§f: §eAlways keep a stack of fresh filter materials in your inventory§f. Once a respirator's filter is exhausted, it will cease to protect you, exposing you instantly to the toxic effects of the local environment.

§6Late-Game Power: Advanced Solar Panels§f

§aHarness the infinite, radiant energy of the stars to power your late-game facilities!§f
While the mod starts in the steam era, it also introduces ultra-high-tier solar generators. Built as advanced upgrades to NuclearCraft's basic energy systems, these massive panels output enormous streams of passive, maintenance-free RF power during daytime.

§6Tiered Solar Arrays§f:
As you advance your tech level, you can craft increasingly dense, quantum-focused solar collectors:
§b• Quantum Solar Panel§f (Tier 1) → §bHyper§f → §bFusion§f → §bNova Solar Panel§f.
§b• Stellar Solar Array§f → §bPhoton§f → §bNebula§f → §bGraviton Solar Harvester§f.
Each progressive tier scales your passive generation capacity exponentially, allowing you to sustain massive server clusters, advanced shielding, or nuclear processing lines completely off the main power grid!

§6Crafting Core§f:
§eUpgrading these panels requires the §bCore of Transformation§f, an advanced, highly irradiated component that focuses light rays and solar waves. Ensure your manufacturing base has access to high-tier NuclearCraft alloys and stable radiation shielding before attempting to craft them.

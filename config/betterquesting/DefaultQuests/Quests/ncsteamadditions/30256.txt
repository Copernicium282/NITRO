§6Steam Age: Industrial Processing§f

§aRun heavy processing machinery on pure, raw steam pressure without needing electricity!§f
NuclearCraft Additions introduces steam-powered versions of classic manufacturing blocks. Running entirely on pressurized steam, these machines allow you to crush ores, compact sheets, and transform chemical compounds long before you build your first high-output generators.

§6Steam Processing Suite§f:
§b• Steam Compactor§f: Uses physical hydraulic pressure to compress materials, allowing you to manufacture advanced metal sheets, plates, and dense plates.
§b• Steam Crusher§f: Grinds down ores and rocks into dusts, giving you basic ore-doubling capabilities early on.
§b• Steam Transformer & Fluid Transformer§f: Processes up to four inputs into advanced chemical products and high-tier alloy components.
§b• Steam Washer & Steam Blender§f: Purifies dusts and blends components to create specialized composite materials.

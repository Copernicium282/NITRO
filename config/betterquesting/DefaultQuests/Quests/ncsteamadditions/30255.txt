§6Steam Age: Power & Boiler Generation§f

§aHarness the pressure of superheated water to jumpstart your industrial energy grid!§f
The §9NuclearCraft Additions: Steam Age§f extension expands your early-game capabilities with specialized thermal machinery. Instead of relying on advanced radioactive fuels early on, you can utilize solid fuels and water to generate high-pressure steam and convert it into passive RF electricity.

§6Core Power Machinery§f:
§b• Steam Boiler§f: The energetic foundation of the Steam tier. It requires a constant supply of §bwater§f and solid §bfuel§f (such as coal or charcoal) to operate. Once active, it boils water into high-pressure §bsteam§f, which can be piped directly into active processing machines or storage.
§b• Steam Turbine§f: The power generator of the Steam tier. Pipe steam directly into the turbine to spin its rotors, generating a steady stream of §eRF/FE electricity§f to power your early-game machines and electrical grids.

§6Zinc Metallurgy§f:
§eSmelt §bZinc Ore§e into §bZinc Ingots§f. Zinc is the primary structural metal required to manufacture steam-era boilers, pipes, and machinery casings, making exploration and mining for zinc essential in the early game.
